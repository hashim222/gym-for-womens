about us

Women of all backgrounds are invited to our gym so that they can stay in shape without feeling uncomfortable. Whether you are a beginner or expert, you will love the environment. If you are a beginner, we provide you with fema certified personal trainers. We offer a range of facilities for women that can be found in the mixed gym to meet that need.

We highly recommend that you become a member so that you can have access to these facilities. Feel free to contact us via the phone number at the bottom of the contact page, or come in person to register with us.
